Mammoth Cave National Park

Things to do

Cave Tours

You can take a range of guided cave tours. There are many beautiful sights inside the cave, all of which you can explore through different tours. There is a historic tour, which you can learn about the history behind the cave dating back to the 1800's. There are more intense tours, such as going spelunking with one of our experts!

Horseback Riding

Aside from exploring the cave and nature trails, you can also go horseback riding. You can book a guided tour or even go camping with your horse in select campgrounds.
